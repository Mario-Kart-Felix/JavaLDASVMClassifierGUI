In January 2016, the  Forum on Cyber Resilience  hosted a workshop on Data Breach Aftermath and Recovery for Individuals and Institutions. Participants examined existing technical and policy remediations, and they discussed possible new mechanisms for better protecting and helping consumers in the wake of a breach. Speakers were asked to focus on data breach aftermath and recovery and to discuss ways to remediate harms from breaches. This publication summarizes the presentations and discussions from the workshop.     Exploring Encryption and Potential Mechanisms for Authorized Government Access to Plaintext: Proceedings of a
