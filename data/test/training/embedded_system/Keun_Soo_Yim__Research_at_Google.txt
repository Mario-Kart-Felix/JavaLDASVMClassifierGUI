My current research interests are in validation, evaluation, and design techniques for dependable computer systems. I hold >15 United States patents on embedded system management methods and apparatuses, and have published >20 technical papers. I received a Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My personal page has all the paper and slide files, and a full patent listing.
